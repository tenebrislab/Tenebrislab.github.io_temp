About

Tenebrism
ˈtɛnəbrɪz(ə)m/
noun
noun: tenebrism
(dramatic illumination) a style of painting developed
by Caravaggio and other 17th-century
Spanish and Italian artists, characterized
by predominantly dark tones and shadows
with dramatically contrasting effects of light.

Tenebris Lab is a CGI VR/MR Design Collaborative, Software and Games Development Studio situated in Gardens, Cape Town.
Born from the concept of bringing Light To Data, we specialize in cross-platform software solutions with VR/MR at its core.
